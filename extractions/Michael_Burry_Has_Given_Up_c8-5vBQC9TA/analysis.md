## The Depreciation Fraud Mechanism

### How It Works

1. **Normal accounting**: Spread capital expense over "useful life" of asset
2. **The manipulation**: Extend "useful life" beyond reality
3. **The effect**: Lower annual depreciation expense = higher reported profits
4. **The scale**: $176 billion understated by 2028 (Burry's estimate)

### Evidence of Manipulation

**Meta's Timeline**:
- 2020: 3-year useful life
- 2024: 5.5-year useful life
- **Result**: Immediate profit boost in the year of change

**Industry Pattern**:
- All major tech companies extending GPU lifespans simultaneously
- From 3 years → 4 years → 5 years → 6 years
- Always in the profit-boosting direction, never the reverse

**Reality Check**:
- Nvidia CEO jokes old GPUs are worthless
- Resale prices falling rapidly
- 2-3 year product cycles in practice
- Companies can't rent out old GPUs at previous prices

### The Future Reckoning

When GPUs actually need replacing every 2-3 years instead of claimed 5-6 years:
1. Companies face massive unexpected capital expenses
2. Reported profits were artificially inflated
3. Real economics of AI infrastructure revealed
4. Potential cascade effect on entire tech sector
